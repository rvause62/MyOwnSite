What's inside

🔑

Self-Custody Wallets

BIP-39 seed, BIP-44 derivation. Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and EVM L2s. Keys encrypted with Argon2id + AES-256-GCM — decrypted only at sign time, wiped immediately after.

💱

DeFi Trading

Swap any token on Uniswap v3. Supply and borrow on Aave v3. Cross-chain swaps via THORChain. All on-chain, no API keys, no middleman.

💳

Spend Anywhere

MetaMask Card — Mastercard debit funded by your wallet. 0% fee on USDC. Works at 150M+ merchants, Apple Pay, Google Pay, ATMs.

📈

Market Cockpit

Live prices from Chainlink oracles. Canvas charts with indicators. Watchlists, portfolio overlay, and one-click quick trade — all custom-drawn, no charting library.

📋

Tax Engine

Local tax computation — FIFO lot tracking, cost basis, wash sale detection, IRS Form 8949 export. Your data never leaves your machine.

🤖

Agent Trading

API tokens for automated bots. TradeAgent (AI-driven) connects via bearer token — MyOwn handles signing. Full audit trail, instant kill switch.

🎯

Limit Orders

Set a target price. MyOwn watches the market and executes the swap when it hits. Funds stay in your wallet until the moment of execution.

🛡

Inheritance

Shamir Secret Sharing — split your seed into N shares requiring K to reconstruct. Distribute to trusted people. Cryptographic dead man's switch.

⛓

Multi-Chain

Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Linea, Bitcoin, Solana. All from one seed. L2 gas under a penny. ERC-20 tokens are config — add any in seconds.
